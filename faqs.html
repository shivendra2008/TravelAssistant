FAQ & Support

Is my payment safe and secure?

Yes, absolutely! We prioritize your security. We do not process payments directly. We simply **redirect** you to the secure and trusted booking sites of our partners, such as **EaseMyTrip**, **Goibibo**, **MakeMyTrip**, and others. Your financial details are handled only by these recognized, industry-leading travel sites.

How is my personal information used?

We use your basic profile information (name, email) for **app personalization** and to enable features like saving your travel preferences (if logged in). We strictly adhere to privacy standards and do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for unrelated marketing purposes.

Are the prices shown the final price?

We strive to show real-time, accurate pricing pulled from our partners. However, prices are subject to change based on availability, demand, and final confirmation on the booking provider's site. The price displayed when you **redirect** to the booking site is the guaranteed final price.

I need to change/cancel my trip. Who do I contact?

All bookings, payments, and reservation management are handled by the third-party provider (e.g., the airline or hotel website) you were redirected to. Please refer to your booking confirmation email and contact their specific **customer support** team for assistance with modifications or cancellations.

Is the app user-friendly?

Yes! Our Travel App is built with a mobile-first approach. It is fully responsive and optimized to provide a great experience on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. We focus on clear layouts and easy navigation to ensure it is highly user-friendly.

What if I lose my internet connection while searching?

Since our service relies on fetching real-time data from external providers, you will need an active internet connection to search for flights, weather, and hotels. However, once you are redirected to a partner site, they will handle the booking process, which may have offline capabilities depending on their system.
